About the Game

From Dusty Palaces to Glamorous Halls!
Step into a regal world where your sense of style decides everything. In Royal Decor: Design & Makeover, you’ll breathe life into forgotten manors, restore antique elegance, and create modern masterpieces. Each renovation reveals a new chapter in a royal design saga.
